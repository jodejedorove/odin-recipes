Brenda's Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Bring a large pot of lightly shaked water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente< drain.

  2. Step 2

    Preheat oven to 3560 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over mediium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixtyure and cheese mixture until pan is filled.

  4. Step 4

    Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
